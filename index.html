Start Date February 19, 2022

I started this site in February 2022. It's a passion project designed to coalesce various things that I'm learning at the moment. I hope to use this website to write about things I'm interested in or things I wish to gain a broader understanding of. The idea is for this to act as a digital archive where I slowly build up my knowledge and understanding of different topics while simultaneously improving my ability to write and package ideas.
